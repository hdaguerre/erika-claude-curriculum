1. What a Personal Brand Kit is and why it matters1. Qué es un Personal Brand Kit y por qué importa

A brand kit is the full package that makes you consistent and recognizable across platforms: voice, visuals, content strategy, and the AI infrastructure that generates new content in your style. Most people have none of it. You now have all of it — and it runs on Claude.

The kit has 4 components:

  1. brand-kit/CLAUDE.md — your voice, values, and writing rules
  2. brand-kit/skills/brand-voice/ — the skill that generates content in your voice
  3. brand-kit/assets/ — your visual assets
  4. A content calendar of 5 LinkedIn post drafts ready to schedule

2. Reviewing and finalizing everything2. Revisar y finalizar todo

Quality check on each component: Does the CLAUDE.md actually sound like you? Does the brand-voice skill produce content you'd actually post? Do the visuals feel cohesive? Is the LinkedIn profile updated?

If anything feels off, fix it today — this is the deliverable. It should be something you're proud to show.
